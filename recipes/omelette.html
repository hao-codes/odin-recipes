Cheese Omelette

Description

A simple recipe for making a delicous cheese omelette

Ingredients
  • 2 Eggs
  • Grated Cheese
  • Salt
  • Minced Garlic
Instructions:
  1. Mix both eggs in a bowl with a fork
  2. Turn the stove on medium heat
  3. Put the eggs in the pan
  4. Put the cheese in the egg mixture when it's still kinda liquid
  5. Put salt and garlic in it
  6. Flip the omelette, but be careful not to burn it
  7. Take it out when it's cooked on both sides and enjoy

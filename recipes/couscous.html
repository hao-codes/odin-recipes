Couscous with Veggies

Description

A simple couscous meal with delicious vegetables.

Ingredients
  • 150g Couscous
  • 1 Cucumber
  • 1 Tomato
  • Salt
Instructions:
  1. Bowl 300 ml water in a kettle
  2. Cut the cucumber and the tomato in small pieces
  3. Put the couscous in a big bowl and pour the hot water in it
  4. Cover the bowl with pot lid or something similar
  5. Wait until the couscous has absorbed all the water, then put the veggies in
  6. Season the meal with salt/pepper
  7. Enjoy! :)
